What it is

The CIS Critical Security Controls (v8.1, 2024) — a short, ranked list of defensive actions, ordered by how much they cut real-world risk. Not "everything you could do" but what to do first.

Distilled from attack data — the highest-payoff defenses, in priority order.

06
Controls ≠ Benchmarks — two different CIS resources · don't conflate them
🧭
CIS Controls — WHAT to do, prioritized. The program-level defensive checklist (this poster).
🔧
CIS Benchmarks — HOW to harden a specific system. Config baselines for OSes, cloud, apps.

Controls tell you to "securely configure assets" (Control 4); Benchmarks give you the exact settings to do it.

07
