01
What it is

A globally-accessible, curated knowledge base of adversary Tactics, Techniques & Procedures (TTPs) — distilled from real-world observations of how attacks actually unfold.

The defender's shared map of how attackers behave — a common language across teams and tools.

02
TTPs, decoded
🎯
Tactic — the WHY: the adversary's goal (e.g. Persistence).
🛠️
Technique — the HOW: a way to reach it. Sub-technique = a specific variant.
🔩
Procedure — a real-world implementation seen in an incident.

Every technique has an ID — e.g. T1566 Phishing.

03
The attack lifecycle — the 14 Enterprise tactics, in order (columns of the matrix)

Not a strict script — attackers loop back and revisit tactics as they go. The columns give a full picture of an intrusion, end to end.

04
The matrices
EnterpriseMobileICS

One knowledge base, three technology domains — Enterprise (IT / cloud), Mobile, and ICS (industrial control systems).

Adversary behavior against AI systems lives in a separate MITRE knowledge base — ATLAS.

Watch out
✅
It's not a checklist — it's a knowledge base, not a compliance to-do list.
📄
Paper ≠ real — coverage on the map ≠ a working detection in prod.
🎚️
Map it to YOU — tailor to your threat model, tech stack & likely adversaries.
